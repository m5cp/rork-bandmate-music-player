# BandMate – Sheet Music Player

## Features

- **Scan sheet music** — Take a photo of sheet music using the camera, or upload an image/PDF from your photo library or files
- **AI-powered music recognition** — Sheet music images are analyzed by AI to detect notes, time signatures, key signatures, and tempo markings
- **Instrument playback** — Hear your sheet music played back with realistic MIDI instrument sounds: Flute, Clarinet, Trumpet, Saxophone, Trombone, or Percussion
- **Playback controls** — Play, pause, adjust tempo with a haptic slider, and loop sections
- **Save & organize** — Songs are saved to the cloud so you can access them across devices; see recently played and all saved songs
- **Practice reminders** — Optional daily reminder with encouraging messages to keep practicing
- **Settings** — Choose your preferred instrument, toggle dark mode, access privacy policy, terms of use, and legal disclaimers

## Design

- **Adaptive theme** — Follows system light/dark mode with a clean blue accent color
- **Apple-native feel** — Uses SF Symbols throughout, system fonts with varied weights, semantic colors, and native controls
- **Tab-based navigation** — 3 tabs: Home, Library, Settings
- **Cards & sections** — Home screen uses grouped cards for quick actions (Scan, Upload) and a "Recently Played" horizontal scroll
- **Player screen** — Sheet music image preview at top, large play/pause button centered, instrument picker as horizontal chips, tempo slider with haptic feedback
- **Smooth animations** — Spring animations on buttons, symbol effects on playback controls, staggered list animations

## Screens

- **Home** — Hero greeting, two action cards (Scan Music, Upload Music), Recently Played horizontal carousel
- **Library** — All saved songs in a searchable list with swipe-to-delete, empty state when no songs saved
- **Scan/Upload** — Camera capture view (with simulator placeholder) or document/photo picker for images and PDFs
- **Processing** — Loading screen while AI analyzes the sheet music image, with animated progress indicator
- **Results** — Parsed music summary (detected key, time signature, tempo, note count), sheet music preview, Play button, Save button
- **Player** — Full playback experience: sheet image, play/pause, tempo slider, instrument selector, loop toggle, progress indicator
- **Settings** — Preferred instrument picker, practice reminder toggle with time picker, About section with legal links and disclaimer

## App Icon

- A deep blue gradient background with a white musical note symbol combined with a subtle camera lens element, conveying "scan and play music" — clean and modern
